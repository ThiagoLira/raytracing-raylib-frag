3. **Ambient occlusion** — Monte Carlo hemisphere sampling with `cosineWeightedHemisphere()`, configurable radius/strength
4. **Emissive materials** — material type 2, adds `emission * emissionStrength` scaled by throughput, terminates ray
5. **Soft shadows** — jittered shadow rays toward random points on light area (controlled by `lightRadius`)

7. **Refraction** — Snell's law via GLSL `refract()`, Schlick Fresnel approximation, total internal reflection detection, stochastic reflect/refract branching
8. **Tone mapping + gamma** — Reinhard `C/(1+C)`, ACES filmic curve, gamma correction `C^(1/2.2)` as final pipeline
